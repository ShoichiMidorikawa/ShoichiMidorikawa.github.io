Joint Probability Density Function

Shoichi Midorikawa

0. Dirac delta function

\[ \delta\left( \varphi(x) \right) = \sum_i \frac{1}{|\varphi'(\alpha_i)|}\delta(x-\alpha_i) \]

　where \(\alpha_i\) is a solution of \( \varphi(x)=0 \).

1. \(\bf Z=X+Y\)

\begin{eqnarray*} f_Z(z) &=& \int \delta(z-x-y)f_X(x)f_Y(y)\, dx\, dy \\ &=& \int f_X(z-y) f_Y(y)\, dy \end{eqnarray*}

2. \(\bf Z=X-Y\)

\begin{eqnarray*} f_Z(z) &=& \int \delta(z-x+y)f_X(x)f_Y(y)\, dx\, dy \\ &=& \int f_X(z+y) f_Y(y)\, dy \end{eqnarray*}

3. \(\bf Z=XY\)

\begin{eqnarray*} f_Z(z) &=& \int \delta(z-xy)f_X(x)f_Y(y)\, dx\, dy \\ &=& \int \frac{1}{|y|}f_X(z/y) f_Y(y)\, dy \end{eqnarray*}

4. \(\bf Z=X^Y\)

\begin{eqnarray*} f_Z(z) &=& \int \delta(z-x^y)f_X(x)f_Y(y)\, dx\, dy \\ &=& \int \frac{1}{|yz^{1-1/y}|}f_X\left(z^{1/y}\right) f_Y(y)\, dy \end{eqnarray*}
